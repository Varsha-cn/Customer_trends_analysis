# Customer_trends_analysis
Consumer Shopping Behavior Analysis | End-to-End Data Analytics Project using Python, SQL, and Power BI to uncover customer purchasing patterns, loyalty drivers, and business growth opportunities.

## Project Overview

This project analyzes consumer shopping behavior data to help a retail company understand purchasing patterns, improve customer engagement, optimize marketing strategies, and increase customer loyalty.

Using Python for data preparation, SQL for business analysis, and Power BI for visualization, the project delivers actionable insights into customer demographics, spending habits, product preferences, seasonal trends, and factors influencing repeat purchases.

## Business Problem

A leading retail company wants to better understand customer shopping behavior across demographics, product categories, and sales channels.

The primary business question is:

**How can the company leverage consumer shopping data to identify trends, improve customer engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies?**

Key areas of focus include:

* Customer segmentation
* Purchase behavior analysis
* Loyalty and repeat purchase patterns
* Product category performance
* Seasonal and promotional impact
* Online vs. offline shopping trends
* Payment preferences and customer satisfaction
